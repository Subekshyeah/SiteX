How to adjust feature weights based on a user's business profile. A budget café weights student foot traffic higher — implement this as a weight vector multiplied against the feature vector before scoring.

SHAP values for XGBoost explainability
SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) gives a per-feature contribution score for each prediction. These values are what get passed to Gemini for the plain-language explanation.

MAE, RMSE, R² and how to use them to compare XGBoost vs HeteroGAT performance. Also: cross-validation strategy for a small geographic dataset.
